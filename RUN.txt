UserScale: GPU-Aware Kubernetes Autoscaler
===========================================

QUICK START:
1. python3 verify_gpu.py
2. python3 setup.py
3. python3 demo.py
4. python3 monitor.py

CLEANUP:
python3 setup.py --cleanup

MINIKUBE PERSISTENT GPU:
minikube start --driver=docker --cpus=2 --memory=2048 --docker-opt=default-runtime=nvidia

MONITORING:
watch -n 1 nvidia-smi
kubectl get pods -n userscale -w
kubectl logs -n userscale deployment/userscale-scaler -f
